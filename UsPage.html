Luke Hemmings

The lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist of 5SOS, Luke is known for his smooth vocals and laid-back personality. Often the tallest in the room, he's a fan of cozy nights and good coffee. Fun fact: Luke started out posting covers on YouTube before the band took off!

Calum Hood

The bassist and backing vocalist of 5SOS, Calum is a multi-instrumentalist with a knack for songwriting. Though he's a bit more reserved, his humour and love for animals make him a fan favourite. Fun fact: Calum initially planned to play soccer before diving into music full-time.
